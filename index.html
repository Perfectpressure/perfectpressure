Frequently Asked Questions

Is Soft Washing Safe For Plants And Landscaping?

Yes, our soft washing process uses Perfect Pressure-approved cleaning solutions that are safe for your plants and landscaping when applied correctly. We take precautions to protect your vegetation during the cleaning process.

How Often Should I Have My House Washed?

We recommend having your house washed annually to maintain its appearance and protect your investment. However, frequency may vary based on your local environment and exposure to elements.

What's Included In House Washing Service?

Our house washing service includes cleaning of exterior siding, removal of dirt, mildew, and algae. This helps maintain your home's appearance and extends the life of your exterior surfaces.

Do You Offer Emergency Cleaning Services?
